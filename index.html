Who We Are

We believe that the best ideas deserve to be heard. Moon Publishing Media is a small, independent publisher with a singular focus: creating content that supports real people in living healthier, more intentional lives.

From guided practices to reflective reading, everything we publish is rooted in care — for the reader, the craft, and the quiet moments that matter most. More is on the way.

Meet Stream Reader

Our first app, Stream Reader, is a calmer way to read: it moves text past you one line at a time, at a pace you set, so it's easier to stay with the words. It's free on the App Store.
