Overview

Stream Reader is built on a simple principle: your reading is your business. The app does not collect, transmit, or share any personal data.

Data Storage

All data generated by your use of Stream Reader — including saved articles, reading settings, and reading progress — is stored locally on your device. None of this data is sent to Moon Publishing Media or any third party. Deleting the app removes all associated data from your device.

Data We Do Not Collect

We do not collect personal information of any kind, usage data or analytics, device identifiers, location data, or crash reports and diagnostics.

Third-Party Services

Stream Reader does not use any third-party analytics, advertising, or tracking services.

Children's Privacy

Stream Reader does not knowingly collect data from anyone, including children under 13.
